All claims below are true today: under 60 seconds, 17+ languages, GDPR, AES 256 GCM encryption,

## English

**Name:** Medyra
**Subtitle (App Store, 30 chars):** Understand medical reports
**Short description (Play, 80 chars):** Medical reports explained in plain language. Private, GDPR compliant.

**Description:**

Medical documents are written for doctors. Medyra explains them to you.

Photograph any medical letter, lab report, or health insurance document and we explain every
value in plain language in under 60 seconds. No medical degree required.

WHAT MEDYRA DOES
- Explains lab values, doctor letters, and Krankenkasse documents in plain language
- Flags which values are normal and which deserve a conversation with your doctor
- Tracks lab values over time in health profiles for you and your family
- Works in 17+ languages

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
- Your data is encrypted with AES 256 GCM before it is stored
- Reports are automatically deleted after 30 days
- GDPR compliant, built and hosted for the EU
- We never sell or share your health data

FREE TO START
3 free reports per month. Personal (4.99 EUR/month) and Family (9.99 EUR/month) add more
reports, health profiles, and trends over time. One subscription works on web and mobile.

Medyra is an educational tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment
recommendations. Always consult your licensed physician before making health decisions.

Supported by Potsdam Transfer, the startup service of the University of Potsdam.

Medizinische Dokumente sind für Ärzte geschrieben. Medyra erklärt sie Ihnen.

WAS MEDYRA KANN
- Erklärt Laborwerte, Arztbriefe und Krankenkassen Dokumente in einfacher Sprache

- Ihre Daten werden vor der Speicherung mit AES 256 GCM verschlüsselt

- Wir verkaufen oder teilen Ihre Gesundheitsdaten niemals

KOSTENLOS STARTEN
3 kostenlose Berichte pro Monat. Personal (4,99 EUR/Monat) und Family (9,99 EUR/Monat) bieten
